## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Track your tennis journey with Tennis Tracker - the ultimate app for tennis players who want to improve their game through data-driven insights.

<b>TRACK YOUR MATCHES</b>
Log every match you play with detailed information:
• Opponent name
• Match format (1 set, 2 sets, or 3 sets with super tiebreak)
• Score tracking with automatic win/loss calculation
• Court type (Clay or Hard court)
• Location where you played
• Match date

<b>COMPREHENSIVE STATISTICS</b>
Get valuable insights into your performance:
• Overall win/loss record and win rate
• Monthly performance tracking
• Current win streak counter
• Last 5 matches overview
• Head-to-head records against each opponent
• Most beaten opponents
• Toughest opponents analysis

<b>PLAYER PROFILE</b>
Create your complete tennis profile:
• Personal information (name, age)
• Playing preferences (dominant hand, racket type)
• Bilingual support (English and Serbian)
• Cloud sync across devices

<b>SMART REMINDERS</b>
Never forget to log your matches:
• Daily reminder notifications
• Customizable reminder time
• String replacement reminder after 4 matches
• Tennis-specific maintenance tips

<b>CLOUD SYNC</b>
Your data is always safe:
• Automatic backup to the cloud
• Access your stats from anywhere
• Secure authentication
• Real-time data synchronization

<b>BILINGUAL SUPPORT</b>
Full support for English and Serbian languages - switch anytime in settings.

Whether you're a competitive player tracking tournament results or a recreational player monitoring your progress, Tennis Tracker helps you stay organized and motivated. Download now and start tracking your tennis journey!
